Privacy Policy

Your Data. Our Responsibility.

At GrowthMetricMedia, transparency and trust are the foundation of everything we do. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your personal information when you interact with our website, forms, or services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, business name.
  • Project-related Information: Requirements, goals, and project details you share.
  • Usage Data: Device info, browser type, IP address, and interaction data to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Respond to inquiries and project requests
  • Send messages & project updates via email or WhatsApp

3. How We Protect Your Information

Your privacy matters. All information submitted through forms is transmitted securely, never sold, traded, or shared with unaffiliated third parties.

We use secure platforms (Zoho Mail, WhatsApp Business API, Google Analytics, etc.) to manage data responsibly.

4. Third-Party Integrations

We may integrate third-party tools such as WhatsApp, analytics tracking tools, or payment services. These platforms have their own privacy policies, and we do not control how they process data.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies to:

  • Analyze website traffic
  • Track user behavior for improving UX
  • Deliver relevant ads on social media & Google

You can disable cookies anytime from your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You can request:

  • Access to your stored data

  • Deletion of your data from our systems

Simply contact us, and we’ll process it within 7 working days.

By submitting a form on our website, you consent to receive communication via WhatsApp, email, or phone.
